Use this screen to choose a song to import.

Navigate with the Up and Down arrow keys.

Press ENTER or SPACE on a folder to open it.

Press ENTER or SPACE on a file to choose it to import. The following file extensions are recognized as importable songs:
 -.MID; .OGG; .MP3; .BAM; .MOD; .SM;
  .IT; .S3M 

Press ESC to cancel and return to the Song Menu.

Some default songs are provided for you in the "import" folder.
